Exercise 4.1 – Conceptual questions
❖ Answer the following questions:
A) What is a Thesaurus?
solution:-A Thesaurus is a resource:
			➢ created for humans and is not a source of perfect information about word relations;
			➢ in which words with similar meanings are grouped together. 
B) Define WordNet’s Synset.
Solution:-
SynSet: Basic unit of WordNet:
• A group of synonymous words which refer to a common semantic concept
• Words may belong to more than one synset – first sense is the most frequent sense
• Words also include collocations (“eye contact”, “mix up”).

C) What is an Ontology?
Solution:  An ontology defines a set of representational primitives with which to model a
domain of knowledge or discourse.
